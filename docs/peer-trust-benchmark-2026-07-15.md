# Buyer-trust peer benchmark — 2026-07-15

This note records the official peer pages reviewed for SCOTTCHEN's evidence,
commercial-terms, case-study and packaging information architecture. Peer claims
are benchmarks only; they are not SCOTTCHEN evidence and must never be copied as
SCOTTCHEN facts.

## Evidence and certification structure

- KLINGSPOR memberships and certifications:
  https://www.klingspor.com/memberships-certifications
  - Useful pattern: organize certificates by legal entity/facility, issuer,
    validity and original-document link.
  - SCOTTCHEN rule: a management-system certificate never proves an exact SKU,
    formulation, rated speed or market requirement by itself.

## Commercial terms and private-label workflow

- NovoAbrasive B2B private labels:
  https://novoabrasive.com/en/b2b-private-labels/
  - Useful pattern: publish MOQ, timing, sample/design scope and Incoterms in one
    process page, alongside legal-entity and address information.
  - SCOTTCHEN rule: do not copy NovoAbrasive's numbers. Use the dated SKU-level
    quotation and contract as the only source of commercial commitments.

- MABTOOLS private labels:
  https://www.mabtools.eu/en/private-labels.php
  - Useful pattern: reference sample, target formulation/specification, safety
    labeling and test validation form one approval chain.

## Packaging, labels and barcodes

- Flexovit private label:
  https://www.flexovitabrasives.com/private-label/
  - Useful pattern: separate product labels, barcodes, pressed labels, retail
    packs and carton marks instead of treating “custom packaging” as one field.

- Bullard private label:
  https://bullardabrasives.squarespace.com/private-label
  - Useful pattern: signed artwork, warnings, scannable codes and non-standard
    packaging costs are explicit release controls.
  - SCOTTCHEN rule: production barcodes come from the buyer's current system;
    mockups and generated visuals must never contain a production-ready code.

## Anonymous case-study evidence

- Norton automotive-finishing success story:
  https://www.nortonabrasives.com/en-gb/resources/document/nw-success-story-rapid-finish-convolute-automotive-finishing-poland
  - Useful pattern: an anonymous case can still publish country, industry,
    application, equipment, exact product, comparison baseline and quantified
    outcome.
  - SCOTTCHEN rule: do not publish a country, timeline, result or testimonial
    without client permission and retained supporting evidence.

## Implementation outcome

The site now separates four states: public, project-specific, not supplied and
AI-generated concept. It also publishes downloadable intake templates for the
evidence register, commercial terms, packaging release and client-approved case
data. Generated visuals are disclosed as concepts and cannot be used as factory,
laboratory, certificate, order or customer proof.
